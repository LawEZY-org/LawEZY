# 🚀 What is LawEZY?

LawEZY is a multi-service legal-tech platform designed to bridge citizens with verified professionals through a secure and intelligent ecosystem.

The platform combines:
- Real-time communication
- Institutional wallet governance
- AI legal assistance
- Professional verification
- Secure cloud architecture

into one seamless digital infrastructure.

# 🌉 Problems We Solve

## 💰 Economic Barrier
Traditional legal retainers are expensive and inaccessible.

### ✅ Solution
A per-minute wallet-based consultation system enables affordable micro-consultations.

---

## 📚 Knowledge Barrier
Most citizens do not understand their legal standing before approaching a lawyer.

### ✅ Solution
LawinoAI acts as a legal first responder by providing contextual legal literacy and research assistance.

---

## 🌍 Geographic Barrier
Quality legal experts are concentrated in urban centers.

### ✅ Solution
LawEZY connects citizens with verified experts regardless of physical location.

|---|---|---|
| Client Portal | React + Vite | High-fidelity institutional UI |

| AI Layer | FastAPI + Gemini | Indian legal intelligence |
| Database | PostgreSQL + MongoDB Atlas | Structured and archival storage |
